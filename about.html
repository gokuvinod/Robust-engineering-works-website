About Us

A revolutionary team

One of the founders, Mr. K.J. Thomas, a machine operator, assembled a team of 15 people and marked the inception of Robust Engineering, previously known as Lunar Plastic in the year 1973. Our primary business was pressure die casting and we used to cater to clients like Siemens, Bajaj and Premier Robust Engineering is a high volume, custom plastic injection moulding supplier. We offer a turnkey and innovative manufacturing solution, including mold design, mold building, and high volume parts manufacturing. Our globally competitive prices eliminate the risks of sourcing offshore. We have a specialization in serving the textile and electronics industry, and we are now paving our way into automobile industry, medical and pharmaceutical, and food and beverage industry.
